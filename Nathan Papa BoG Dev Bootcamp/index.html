Bits of Good Application

Hi, I'm Nathan Papa and I'm a first year computer science major. I was born in

California but have lived in Tennessee since I was five years old. For the last five

years, I've spent a lot of my extracurricular time focusing on cybersecurity.

As I learned more about how hackers abuse people's lack of knowledge on the

topic, I wanted to do whatever I could to make sure that I could teach the people

around me how to be as safe as possible online. This is when I became involved with

CEROC (Cybersecurity Education Research and Outreach Center), through Tennessee

Technological University in my hometown. In my time there, I worked on creating

materials to educate students in elementary, middle, and high schools around the

state on best cybersecurity practices. While CEROC only focused on a few of the

people vulnerable to hackers, I'd like to think that my materials may save a few

students, their families, or their friends from being hacked sometime in the future.
